MediFund

Government endowment fund to help needy Singapore Citizens who cannot afford medical bills even after using MediShield Life and MediSave.

Eligibility

  • Singapore Citizen
  • Received subsidized treatment at a public healthcare institution
  • Exhausted other means of payment (MediShield Life, MediSave)
  • Unable to afford remaining bill due to financial difficulty

Assessment Criteria

Factor Consideration
Household Income Per capita household income
Assets Property, savings, investments
Family Support Ability of family to contribute
Medical Bill Size Outstanding bill amount

MediFund Silver

For Singaporeans aged 65 and above. Additional support for elderly patients.

Medication Assistance Fund (MAF)

Provides subsidies for expensive outpatient drugs not fully covered by standard subsidies, including CDL cancer drugs.

How to Calculate PCHI

PCHI = Total Gross Monthly Household Income √∑ Number of Household Members

Example: Family of 4 with total income of $8,000/month

PCHI = $8,000 √∑ 4 = $2,000/month ‚Üí Eligible for up to 75% MAF subsidy

Applying for MAF

  1. Visit Medical Social Worker at the hospital
  2. Complete means test assessment
  3. Provide income documentation
  4. MAF applied automatically to eligible drug bills

Community Health Assist Scheme (CHAS)

Subsidies at participating GP clinics for eligible Singaporeans.

  • Blue Card (household income ‚â§$1,200 PCHI): Higher subsidies
  • Orange Card (household income $1,201-$1,500 PCHI): Standard subsidies
  • Pioneer/Merdeka Generation: Additional subsidies

Pharmaceutical Company Patient Assistance Programs

Many pharmaceutical companies offer assistance programs for expensive drugs:

  • Co-payment assistance
  • Free drug supply for qualifying patients
  • Ask your oncologist or hospital social worker about eligibility
