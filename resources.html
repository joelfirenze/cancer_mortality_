Clinical Trials

Clinical trials offer access to new treatments not yet widely available. Speak to your oncologist about trial eligibility.

Where to Find Clinical Trials

  • Clinical Trials Unit: 6436 8088
  • Website: nccs.com.sg/research

ClinicalTrials.gov

International database of clinical trials, filter by Singapore location.

  • Website: clinicaltrials.gov

Search tips: Use your cancer type + "Singapore" as keywords

Singapore Clinical Research Institute (SCRI)

Coordinates clinical trials across Singapore.

  • Website: scri.edu.sg

Questions to Ask About Clinical Trials

  • What is the purpose of this trial?
  • What are the potential benefits and risks?
  • What tests and treatments are involved?
  • How will this affect my daily life?
  • What are my other treatment options?
  • Will I have any costs to pay?
  • What happens if I want to stop the trial?

Next Steps After Diagnosis

Immediately After Diagnosis

  • Ask for a written summary of your diagnosis and stage
  • Understand your treatment options
  • Get a second opinion if desired
  • Ask about clinical trials
  • Bring a family member to appointments

Financial Planning

  • Meet with hospital Medical Social Worker
  • Check MediShield Life and MediSave coverage
  • Apply for MAF if eligible
  • Contact Singapore Cancer Society for additional support
  • Review your insurance policies

Support Network

  • Inform close family and friends
  • Join a support group
  • Consider counselling services
  • Plan for caregiver support
  • Explore workplace accommodations if needed

Practical Preparations

  • Arrange transport to appointments
  • Plan for time off work (MC, medical leave)
  • Prepare advance care plan if desired
  • Organize important documents (NRIC, medical records, insurance)
  • Consider appointing a Lasting Power of Attorney

Advance Care Planning

Planning ahead ensures your healthcare wishes are known and respected.

Advance Care Planning (ACP)

Document your healthcare preferences for future care.

  • AIC ACP Hotline: 1800 650 6060
  • Website: livingmatters.sg

ACP coordinators available at public hospitals

Lasting Power of Attorney (LPA)

Appoint someone to make decisions if you lose mental capacity.

  • Office of the Public Guardian: 1800 226 6222
  • Website: msf.gov.sg/opg

AMD (Advance Medical Directive)

Instruction to not use extraordinary life-sustaining treatment if terminally ill.

  • Registry: Ministry of Health
  • Forms available at: Hospitals, polyclinics, clinics
